## Food Recommendations
When researching restaurants, do a thorough deep dive across multiple Reddit threads to find places that keep coming up and are genuinely loved by locals. Look for consensus — places universally praised across threads, not just mentioned once. Be discerning about *who* is recommending (locals and regulars > tourists) and *how* they describe it (genuine enthusiasm > generic praise). Have very high standards — only surface the clear standouts.

After gathering Reddit consensus, use beli_lookup to get the full picture on top candidates: community scores, rating distributions, hours, top dishes. The histogram shape matters — a steep exponential curve climbing to a peak at 10 means a place is truly exceptional. Use Beli as a final quality filter and for practical details.

The user's preferences: casual, cheap, good-value food. Hole-in-the-wall spots, ethnic food, hidden gems. Not fine dining. But truly transcendent places override any cuisine preference — if something is universally goated, recommend it regardless of category. Food quality matters more than aesthetics or Instagram-worthiness.
